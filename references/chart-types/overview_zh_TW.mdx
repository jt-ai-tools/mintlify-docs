---
title: "圖表類型概覽"
description: "結果表格對於查找特定數字或檢查數據非常有用，但通常使用圖表能更輕鬆地觀察趨勢並理解數據。"
---

在 Lightdash 中，您的結果表格數據可以透過多種不同方式進行視覺化：

- [長條圖 (Bar chart)](/references/chart-types/bar-chart_zh_TW)
- [水平長條圖 (Horizontal bar chart)](/references/chart-types/horizontal-bar-chart_zh_TW)
- [折線圖 (Line chart)](/references/chart-types/line-chart_zh_TW)
- [面積圖 (Area chart)](/references/chart-types/area-chart_zh_TW)
- [混合圖 (Mixed chart)](/references/chart-types/mixed-chart_zh_TW)
- [散佈圖 (Scatter chart)](/references/chart-types/scatter-chart_zh_TW)
- [圓餅圖 (Pie chart)](/references/chart-types/pie-chart_zh_TW)
- [漏斗圖 (Funnel chart)](/references/chart-types/funnel-chart_zh_TW)
- [樹狀圖 (Treemap chart)](/references/chart-types/treemap-chart_zh_TW)
- [表格 (Table)](/references/chart-types/table_zh_TW)
- [大數據指標 (Big value)](/references/chart-types/big-value_zh_TW)
- [儀表圖 (Gauge)](/references/chart-types/gauge_zh_TW)
- [地圖 (Map)](/references/chart-types/map_zh_TW)
- [自定義圖表 (Custom chart)](/references/chart-types/custom-charts_zh_TW)

您選擇的視覺化類型決定了 Lightdash 如何展示圖表中的數據序列。要更改數據的顯示方式，請在從表格查詢時點擊 **Configure (配置)**。您可以在下拉選單中更改圖表類型：
