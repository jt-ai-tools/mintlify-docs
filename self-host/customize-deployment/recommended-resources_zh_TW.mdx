## lightdash

* **CPU**: 1
* **記憶體 (Memory)**: 1.5Gi
* **臨時儲存 (Ephemeral Storage)**: 1Gi

## browserlessChrome (無頭瀏覽器)

* **CPU**: 2
* **記憶體 (Memory)**: 4Gi
* **臨時儲存 (Ephemeral Storage)**: 1Gi

## scheduler (排程器)

* **CPU**: 1
* **記憶體 (Memory)**: 1425Mi
* **臨時儲存 (Ephemeral Storage)**: 1Gi
